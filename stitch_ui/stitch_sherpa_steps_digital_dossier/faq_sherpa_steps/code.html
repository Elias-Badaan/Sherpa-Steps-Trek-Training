Base Camp Knowledge

High-altitude trekking requires more than just lung capacity. It requires technical discipline, specific gear literacy, and physiological adaptation. Here is how we prepare you for the thin air.

It changes everything. By training in a hypoxic environment (12% oxygen, simulating ~4,000m), your body is forced to produce more red blood cells and increase mitochondrial efficiency. Training at sea level is good for your heart; training in the Altitude Room is good for your blood.
The "Gold Standard" is 16 weeks. This allows for a 4-week base phase, an 8-week intensification phase in the chamber, and a 4-week summit push simulation. However, we have accelerated 8-week tracks for those with existing fitness foundations.
Our protocols are tailored for the "Big Three": Everest Base Camp (Nepal), Kilimanjaro (Tanzania), and the Inca Trail (Peru). We also provide custom periodization for Mont Blanc, Aconcagua, and technical peaks in the Cascades.
Programs start at $299/month. This includes 3x weekly coached Altitude Room sessions, a personalized digital training plan, monthly lactate threshold testing, and a 15% discount on gear through our partners.
