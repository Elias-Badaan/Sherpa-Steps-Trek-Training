12-Week Ascent
THE 12-WEEK PROGRAM

Base, Load,
Then Thin Air

Twelve weeks. Three phases. In that order, for a reason. Each phase builds the body the next one needs — first the legs, then the load, then the oxygen debt you'll meet on the mountain.

FULL 12-WEEK TUITION

Includes all coached stairwell sessions, programmed pack training, and altitude room access. It does not include your trek, gear, or flights — we train the body, you book the mountain.

PHASE 01

Base

WEEKS 1–4

Build aerobic base and unloaded stair volume so the legs can take the work.

SAMPLE SESSION

60-minute continuous stairwell climb, no pack, building from 20 to 45 floors.

PHASE 02

Load

WEEKS 5–8

Add the pack you'll actually carry and train your legs and back under that weight.

SAMPLE SESSION

Weighted stair intervals: 8 floors loaded, 2 floors recovery, pack rising from 20 to 35 pounds.

PHASE 03

Thin Air

WEEKS 9–12

Train in the altitude room to manage oxygen debt so your body adapts before your flight does — and you arrive steady, not surprised.

SAMPLE SESSION

Loaded climb at simulated 4,000m: managed-effort intervals with heart-rate and oxygen-saturation tracking.
