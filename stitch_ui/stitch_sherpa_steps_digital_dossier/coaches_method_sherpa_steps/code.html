System Protocol 01 // Intervals
01

Stairwell Intervals

Vertical displacement is the only metric that matters. We utilize multi-story stairwells with heavy packs to build specific muscular endurance that a treadmill simply cannot replicate.

02

Pack Training

Your body must adapt to the "Third Lung"—your rucksack. Training begins at 10% body weight and scales to 25%, focused on core stability and eccentric leg strength for the descent.

03

Biometric Loyalty

We don't guess. We track heart rate variability, SpO2, and recovery metrics to ensure you're over-reaching without over-training. Precision is our primary safety tool.
